T.H.E Lab

TENTATIVE NAME: The Hopeful Earthling Lab (T.H.E. Lab)

TENTATIVE MISSION STATEMENT: The Hopeful Earthling Lab (T.H.E. Lab): Human psychology is similar in many ways to that of other animals who live on Earth, where biology and evolution play very important roles in explaining behaviours. But earthlings are peculiar animals because humans think and feel in interesting ways! For instance, because of their highly advanced prefrontal cortex, they are capable of higher-order sophisticated thinking skills (involving language, symbolic and abstract thinking, and self-consciousness) which in turn, make them prone of thinking about themselves and others in complex ways! We earthlings are social animals! THE Lab aims to intersect biology and psychology to encourage a broad range of research projects that pertains to human beings. And as emphasized in our lab name, we are hopeful in the future and in the importance of science to contribute to the well-being of humans and their societies. We believe and hope in the capacity of us earthlings to use empirical research to bring about evidence-based interventions to make a better future for all beings on Earth, and beyond!
